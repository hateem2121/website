Design direction B

Raw Material

The honest workshop: undyed cloth, natural-dye colour and craft you can touch. Calm, warm, transparent — sustainability proven by material truth, never by green cliché.

Umber ink — the everyday mark, on ecru or canvas. Quiet, certain.

Madder clay — reserved for campaign moments and large lockups only.

Reversed on umber — footer, woven labels, garment tags.

06 · Motion character

Motion in Raw Material behaves like cloth, not software. Elements drape into place over 400–600 ms on soft ease-out curves — they settle, they never snap. Reveals read as fabric lifted off the cutting table: a rise, a slow landing, done.

Bold on the home hero, the heritage timeline and category landings — full panels slide and settle, headlines rise, the madder seam draws itself across the page. Subtle on forms, the RFQ flow and the buyer portal — a 200 ms fade and a gentle lift, nothing that interrupts the work. Minimal on catalog grids — a soft shadow and a stitch of underline, so forty products stay scannable. Every animation on this page is wrapped in prefers-reduced-motion: no-preference and simply disappears for visitors who ask for stillness.
