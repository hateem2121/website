06 Motion Character

Night Forge moves like calibrated machinery: fast, precise, no bounce. Everything snaps in 150–250ms on cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1) — a hard launch with a clean stop, like a press finishing its stroke. Reveals wipe in on the diagonal, echoing the wordmark's cut angles; numerals tick upward like a production counter; hovers get a single copper edge light, never a soft glow. Motion is bold on the home hero, heritage story and category landings (diagonal wipes, ticking counters, beam sweeps), subtle on forms and the buyer portal (state changes and focus only), and minimal on catalog grids (a lift and an edge light — nothing that races the eye while scanning products). Every animation on this page sits behind prefers-reduced-motion; with motion off, all content renders complete and static.
